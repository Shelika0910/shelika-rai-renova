About Us

We Believe Mental Health is a Right, Not a Privilege

ReNova was founded with a simple mission: make quality mental health care accessible to everyone. We bridge the gap between patients seeking help and certified professionals ready to provide it.

Our platform combines human compassion with modern technology — including AI assistance, resource libraries, and seamless scheduling — to deliver a holistic wellness experience.

  • Verified & certified mental health professionals
  • Secure, private, and HIPAA-aligned practices
  • AI-powered support available 24/7
  • Flexible scheduling — session at your pace
  • Evidence-based therapy methods and resources
